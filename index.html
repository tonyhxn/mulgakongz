How can I get whitelisted?

Details regarding our whitelist are as follows:
2 seperate whitelist's will exist, 2500 Whitelist spots will be finalised
@Beach Bum = 2 Mints for whitelisted members on this whitelist
@OG MulgaKong = 3 Mints for whitelisted members on this upgraded whitelist

In order to certify and secure your whitelist spot, we will be hosting a variety of challenges, games, in real life event's and giveaways!
